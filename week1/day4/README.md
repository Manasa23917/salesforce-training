# Day 4 - Flow Builder

## What is Flow Builder?

Flow Builder is a no-code automation tool in Salesforce used to automate business processes.

---

# Types of Flows

## Screen Flow

A Screen Flow allows users to enter information through screens.

Example:
Student registration form.

---

## Record Triggered Flow

A Record Triggered Flow runs automatically when records are created or updated.

Example:
Auto email after student registration.

---

# Automation Ideas

## 1. Auto email after registration
Helps save manual work and improves communication.

## 2. Generate student ID automatically
Reduces manual effort and speeds up process.

## 3. Auto update remaining seats
Prevents seat calculation mistakes.

## 4. Send fee reminder notification
Improves fee payment tracking.

## 5. Notify faculty when course is full
Helps faculty manage admissions better.

# Manual vs Automated Process

## Manual Process

Staff manually enters student details, checks course seats, and sends emails.

### Problems
- Time consuming
- Human errors
- Delays

---

## Automated Process

Salesforce automation automatically checks seats, updates records, and sends emails.

### Benefits
- Faster process
- Accurate data
- Saves time

---

# Reflection

## Why automation matters in enterprise systems

Automation helps companies save time, reduce errors, improve productivity, and maintain consistency in business processes.

---

# Reflective Questions

## 1. Why do companies automate workflows?

To save time and improve efficiency.

---

## 2. What problems happen with manual processes?

Manual processes can cause delays and errors.

---

## 3. Difference between Screen Flow and Record Triggered Flow?

Screen Flow needs user interaction, while Record Triggered Flow runs automatically.

---

## 4. Why is no-code automation powerful?

Because business processes can be automated without programming knowledge.

---

## 5. When should automation be avoided?

Automation should be avoided for complex decisions requiring human judgment.

---

## 6. How does automation improve consistency and productivity?

Automation performs tasks quickly and reduces manual mistakes.
