# Day 1 - CRM Basics

## What is CRM?
CRM stands for Customer Relationship Management.
It helps companies manage customer data, sales, and communication.

## Why Companies Use Salesforce
Companies use Salesforce to:
- Manage customers
- Track sales
- Improve communication
- Store business data

## Important Concepts

### Account
An organization or company.

### Contact
A person associated with the account.

### Opportunity
A possible business deal or sale.


## Business Workflow

Lead → Contact → Opportunity → Customer

A lead is a person interested in a service.
Their details become a contact.
If business possibility exists, it becomes an opportunity.
After successful completion, they become a customer.


## Real World Mapping - College Admission

Account = College

Contact = Student

Lead = Student asking for admission

Opportunity = Admission process

Customer = Joined student
